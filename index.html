More people than ever are using devices and apps that track the number of steps they take, how long they sleep, what they eat, and how much they work out. At the same time, people are more sedentary than ever, as study after study demonstrates the ill effects of sitting still. Clearly, we need a new way to motivate motion.

Enter The Welle, a game that tracks your progress toward your fitness goals and translates your achievements into stats for an RPG character.

The Welle project includes a cross-platform mobile RPG, but we also plan to create a platform for other developers to build games that incorporate health and fitness data. In other words, by improving your health in the real world, you can level up in games that you don't even play yet. Sound like a win-win? Give us your cash!

Our backing goal of $300k will allow us to:

  • Feed and house ourselves while we build this thing.
  • Buy replacements for the laptops that Gavin spills beverages on.
